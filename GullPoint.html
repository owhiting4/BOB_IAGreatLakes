Gull Point

By Isaac Young, Olivia Whiting, and Tatum Long

In the Gull Point subset data, we see an overall large amount of severity ratings across the entire area.

Green Clusters

Within the green cluster there are high ratings of severity amongst all the points, but they also appear to be far from roads, buildings, and water with a low elevation.

Red Clusters

There is a small cluster of red points that show similarly high severity ratings of 4.5-6.0. They are close to buildings and roads and somewhat close to buildings. They have a small elevation similar to the other cluster.

Overall this data does not show much correlation between roads, buildings, or water. On the other hand, there is somewhat of a trend of low elevation leading to higher severity.
